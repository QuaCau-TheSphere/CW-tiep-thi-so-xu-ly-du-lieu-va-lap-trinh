Khái niệm:: 
- You _should not_ keep your algorithm secret.
- You _do not know_ how much your algorithm is secret.
- You _cannot_ keep your algorithm secret.
- But you can and must keep your password secret, and you can know "how much" secret it is (that's all the "entropy" business).
